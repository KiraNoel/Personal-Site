In the early months of 2020 my humanities class spent time to discuss the Black Lives Matter movement and how we could have a discussion about it in our school. A few friends and I came up with the idea of making a fist out of origami roses. The roses represent the many lives lost to police brutality. We also hosted discussions in our school to foster connection between students of color and teachers and administrators.
